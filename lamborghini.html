Aventador

This is the Lamborghini Aventador and its an amazing car. It has a V12 engine which is naturally aspirated, meaning it does not use a turbo or supercharger to get its power.

This car costs around $400,000

Huracan

This is the Lamborghini Huracan, it is also an amazing car. It is lighter than the Aventador. It also has a smaller V10 engine. It is also naturally aspirated, so no turbo or supercharger.

This car costs around $300,000
